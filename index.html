Discover: The city of Guanajuato is known for its colonial architecture, tunnels, alleys and colorful houses in the hills. Guanajuato is ideal for tourists who enjoy sightseeing, it is possible to walk the narrow streets and travel through time to colonial times.
Eating: Guanajuato is definitely the most representative city of Mexican gastronomy. From food stalls in the San Miguel market, gastronomic festivals through the year, to gourmet restaurants with the most internationally recognized Mexican chefs.

(whithout searching google),and he directed me to other coffee place that had bikes to rent, finally went to an amazing tour to the mountain starting in small town called Santa Rosa.

Discover:The Greek theatre Impressive, solemn, intriguing, with stunning views It may happen that, while sitting on the big stone steps , you hear the voices of the great Greek heroes, Agamemnon, Medea or Oedipus, even if there are no actors on the stage …this is such an evocative place! It keeps evidences of several historic periods, from the prehistoric ages to Late Antiquity and the Byzantine era.
Eating:At the entrance of Ortygia island, that is connected to the city by Umbertino bridge, you will be greeted by the aroma every kinds of spices, chilli peppers from Etna, almond cakes, as well as seafood and fresh fish. A small market enlivened by colourful “vanniate”, catchy phrases in Sicilian dialect.
Sightseeing:If you like castles, you are in the right place: these are among the most picturesque castles of the Island. Maniace Castle is named after the Byzantine Commander Giorgio Maniace

Discover:Perito Moreno Glaciar is one of the main tourist attractions throughout all of Argentina, Perito Moreno Glacier is a place filled with natural beauty and wonder at nearly every glancing turn. The ice formation covers over 250 square km (97 square miles.) The height of the ice is 74 mts(240 ft).
Eating:Cordero Patagonico (Patagonia Lamb bbq) The lamb is a meat used to make bbq in Argentina, but there is a region where it is the favorite: The south (more specifically in Santa Cruz and Chubut). This is because it is there where the best breeding of lambs occurs, thanks to the fact that the climate in that place is dry, and it rains just and necessary.
Outdoors: Trekking on Ice,The Big Ice Perito Moreno glacier trekking tour is the ultimate adventure tour of the famous glacier near El Calafate, Argentina. A unique full day trekking experience . Enjoy spectacular views of the breathtaking ice formations in Argentina’s Los Glaciares National Park.   With its mesmerizing blue lagoons, deep cracks and stunning caves,you won’t believe your eyes as you trek and hike through this dream-like landscape .

Eating:Fill up on tapas, pintxos and vermouth Pintxos, in essence, are Basque tapas - plates of bite-sized goodies served atop a piece of bread - and they're also a culinary trend in Barcelona. Tradition calls for you to pick at the food with toothpicks.You can get stuck in to ham empanadillas (a type of pie).
Discover:Barcelona's most famous street is both a tourist magnet and a window into Catalan culture, with cultural centers, theaters and intriguing architecture. Flanked by plane trees, the middle section of La Rambla is a broad pedestrian boulevard, crowded every day until the wee hours with a wide cross-section of society.
Sightseeing:Picasso Museum. In the Born area. The Picasso Museum is Barcelona's most famous museum. Pablo Picasso was born in Malaga in the south of Spain, but the Picasso family moved to Barcelona in 1895 and Picasso lived here until 1904. Picasso's father was an art teacher and although as a child Pablo Picasso already studied art in Malaga, it was in Barcelona that he spent his formative years.
